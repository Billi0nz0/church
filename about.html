Global Champions Ministries

Our History

Global Champions Ministries was founded in May 2015 by Apostle Ifeanyi Ugochi with a divine mandate to raise champions for Christ and impact lives through the power of the gospel. Since its inception, the ministry has been committed to spreading the message of faith, hope, and transformation across communities. What began as a vision has grown into a vibrant fellowship where believers are equipped, empowered, and inspired to fulfill their God-given destinies.

Our Mission

Global Champions Ministries is a faith-based movement dedicated to raising champions for Christ. Our mission is to empower individuals to live victorious lives, grow in faith, and impact their world with love and integrity. We believe every person has a God-given destiny, and through worship, teaching, mentorship, and outreach, we equip believers to walk in purpose and transform communities. At Global Champions Ministries, we are more than a church—we are a family of champions, united in hope, committed to service, and passionate about changing the world for Christ.

Meet Our Leaders

At Global Champions Ministries, we are blessed with dedicated leaders who serve with vision, integrity, and compassion. Each leader is committed to guiding others in faith, empowering lives, and building a community centered on Christ’s love.
