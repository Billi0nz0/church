What We Believe

We believe in one God Father, Son and Holy-Spirit.

We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives, guide us in truth, and equip us for every good work.

We believe that salvation comes through Jesus Christ, who died and rose again for our redemption.

We believe in the living Word of God as the foundation of our faith.
